This is the shelf for the lived events and experiences that changed how I think, move, or understand my own direction.

## Why this category exists

Not everything important belongs under identity, philosophy, or habit. Some things matter because they happened. A conversation, a setback, a move, a threshold, a period of pressure, a moment that changes the texture of how I see the future. This category is where those things belong.

## What should go here

I want this section to hold concrete experiences with some weight behind them. Not random diary fragments, but events that actually altered perspective, standards, motivation, relationships, or trajectory.

## What it should become

Over time this should read like a map of turning points. The point is not only memory. It is orientation. Experiences are part of how a life becomes legible.
