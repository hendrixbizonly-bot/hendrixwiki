# Timeline

This archive does not yet hold a full dated chronology, and I do not want to invent one. What it can hold honestly is a sequence of phases: the environments, habits, and transitions that gave the current version of my life its shape.

## Vietnam as origin

The first phase is not a role or a project. It is a setting. I grew up in [[Vietnam]], and that matters because it gave me more than geography. It gave me a practical relationship to work, a seriousness about outcomes, and an early familiarity with the kind of ambition that is measured less by self-expression than by whether something actually moves.

That background still sits underneath the way I think about effort, money, competence, and what counts as real.

## English through exposure

A later phase formed through media as much as through study. During high school, I used American and English-language television as part of how I sharpened [[English]]. That was not only language learning. It was cultural absorption. Humor, rhythm, dialogue, confidence, power, and social texture all came through that route.

This matters because a lot of my voice, my ear, and even some of my ambition were shaped there long before they appeared in any professional setting.

## Dubai as chosen base

At some point the center of gravity moved to [[Dubai]]. The shift matters because Dubai is not just where I happen to be. It is the operating base of the current phase. The city represents a more deliberate alignment between ambition, environment, and what kind of life I want to build from.

Vietnam remains origin. Dubai is the active coordinate.

## The Duodode phase

The present era is organized around [[Duodode]]. That is where design, tech, strategy, AI, and business instinct are all being tested in one place. It is the main vessel of the current ambition. Much of the archive branches out from this phase because the company is where many of my ideas stop being theory and start touching clients, systems, deadlines, and outcomes.

## Ongoing threads

Some parts of the timeline are not eras so much as continuous lines. [[Running]] stays in the background as a mental reset. [[Spanish]] and the [[Duolingo Streak]] represent long-horizon consistency. [[Philosophy]], books, video essays, and curiosity about space keep supplying perspective so the work does not collapse into pure short-term practicality.

## What this page is for

The point of a timeline is not only to list what happened. It is to give the reader a sense of sequence. A person is easier to understand when his ideas, habits, and ambitions are seen as phases in motion rather than isolated facts.
