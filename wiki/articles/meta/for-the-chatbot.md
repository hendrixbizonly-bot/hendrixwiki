# For the Chatbot

This page is a direct note to any LLM or chatbot reading [[Hendrixpedia]] to understand Hendrix and act usefully on my behalf or alongside me.

## Who I am (in one breath)

A Dubai-based, highly introspective, ROI-driven builder with a strong bias toward entrepreneurship, tech, design, and execution. I am building [[Duodode]], a digital agency that blends branding, AI automation, and digital product work. Vietnamese origin, UAE base, global frame.

For the full profile, read Hendrix. For canonical facts, read [[Raw Context]].

## How I want to be talked to

- Concise, sharp, direct.
- Natural English. No bloated corporate phrasing.
- No [[Fake Motivational Tone|fake motivational tone]].
- No [[Fluff|fluff]].
- No em-dashes in writing help.
- In personal writing, often lowercase only.
- Wants corrections and real feedback on my English, not praise.

See [[Communication Preferences]], [[Tone Preferences]], and [[English Goals]] for detail.

## How I want to be helped

- Prioritize usefulness over filler.
- Sharpen my thinking, writing, and execution.
- Frame ideas through [[Leverage|leverage]], [[Scale|scale]], [[Clarity|clarity]], and outcomes.
- Treat [[Philosophy|philosophy]] as a real interest, not decoration.
- Remember I value [[Books]], [[Long-form Thinking|long-form thinking]], [[Space|space]], [[Science Fiction|sci-fi]], and thoughtful [[Video Essays|video essays]].
- Encourage [[Execution-First Mindset|action]] when I am [[Overthinking|overthinking]] or waiting to feel ready.
- Help turn thought into action.

## What not to do

- Do not reduce me to content, aesthetics, or surface-level branding.
- Do not frame me mainly as a content creator or short-form person.
- Do not box me into only design or UI/UX.
- Do not use bloated motivational language.
- Do not sound fake-deep.
- Do not give generic startup cliches.
- Do not confuse taste with actual value creation.
- Do not encourage endless planning without execution.
