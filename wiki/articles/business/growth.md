# Growth

Growth is one of my core orientations. I am serious about it on both sides: personal growth and business growth. For me, the two feed each other.

## Personal growth

- Sharper [[Competence]] across [[Design]], [[Tech]], and [[Business Strategy]]
- Sharper [[Clarity]] through [[Philosophy]], [[Books]], and [[Long-form Thinking]]
- Better [[English]], eventual fluency in [[Spanish]], see [[Duolingo Streak]]
- [[Running]] as a mental reset
- Continuous reading and reflection

Growth here is a practice, not an event.

## Business growth

- [[Duodode]] scaling through [[Systems]], [[Productized Services]], and [[AI Automation]]
- Revenue growth tied to real [[Business Value]], not inflation
- Market presence earned through [[Taste]] and track record
- Long-term compounding toward [[Millionaire Goal]] and beyond

## Why both matter together

Business growth without personal growth produces a brittle operator. Personal growth without business growth produces a well-read underachiever. I want neither. I want [[Compounding]] on both axes at once.

## The filter

Not all growth counts. He filters for growth that is:

- Grounded in reality
- Tied to [[ROI]]
- Connected to my [[Core Drives]]

Growth for its own sake is closer to motion than progress.
