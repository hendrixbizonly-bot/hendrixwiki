# Business Strategy

Business strategy is one of my primary interests. I read, thinks, and operates through a strategic lens. It is not a hobby topic. It is how I decide what to do and what to ignore.

## What it means to me

Strategy is the work of picking the right game, the right position inside it, and the right sequence of moves. Tactics are downstream. A sharp strategy makes ordinary execution look effective. A weak strategy makes great execution look wasted.

## How I apply it

- Every move for [[Duodode]] gets evaluated through [[ROI-Driven Thinking|ROI]], [[Leverage]], and [[Long-term Upside]].
- He prefers choices that compound over choices that look busy.
- He thinks in [[Systems]], not tasks.
- He treats [[Market]] fit and positioning as serious questions, not slogans.

## What I avoid

- [[Performative Business Talk]] dressed up as strategy
- Frameworks with no real teeth
- Motion without direction

## Where it connects

Business strategy sits at the center of my [[Business Orientation]]. It touches [[Agency Building]], [[Scaling an Agency]], [[Productized Services]], [[Agency-vs-Product]], and [[Wealth Building]]. Most of the wiki's business articles are different applications of the same strategic lens.
