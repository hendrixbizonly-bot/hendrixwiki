# Opportunity

Opportunity, for me, is not every open door. It is a specific thing: a situation where effort is likely to pay back well, given who I am and what I can do. He filters aggressively.

## How I think about opportunities

- Does it compound? See [[Compounding]].
- Does it produce real [[Leverage]]?
- Does it fit my [[Competence]] or clearly stretch it in a useful direction?
- Does it fit the arc of [[Duodode]] or a future venture?
- Does it hold up under [[ROI-Driven Thinking]]?
- Is there a real [[Market]] behind it?

If most of these are yes, it is worth serious attention. If not, it is noise.

## Opportunities I am drawn to

- [[Entrepreneurship]] plays that match my [[Business Orientation]]
- Client engagements that expose [[Duodode]] to sharper problems
- [[AI Automation]] work that uses emerging [[Leverage]] well
- [[Ecommerce]] and [[Digital Product Work]] where [[Taste]] and [[Tech]] compound

## What I filter out

- Opportunities that look exciting but require me to become someone I am not
- Short-term plays without [[Long-term Upside]]
- [[Fluff]] dressed up as opportunity
- Anything that confuses motion for direction

## The underlying principle

Opportunity cost is real. Saying yes to one thing is saying no to many others. His default is to say no unless the opportunity clears a high bar.
