# Success

Success is a word [[Hendrix]] uses carefully. For him it is not a vibe or a social signal. It has a specific shape.

## How he defines it

Success is the combination of:

- Building something real, not just performative
- Becoming strong and capable across multiple surfaces
- Creating financial outcomes that match the ambition, see [[Millionaire Goal]] and [[Wealth Building]]
- Avoiding [[Mediocrity]]
- Living with intention
- Turning thought into action, see [[Build and Ship]]

See [[Core Drives]].

## What success is not

- Not an aesthetic
- Not a follower count
- Not a title
- Not borrowed from someone else's definition

He is specifically allergic to [[Performative Business Talk]] and success framed as lifestyle branding.

## Why it matters

He cares about success because mediocrity is the default outcome of an unintentional life. A clear definition of success forces every decision to be evaluated against something real. Without it, effort drifts toward whatever is easiest to signal.

## How it connects

Success sits at the intersection of [[Ambition]], [[Money]], [[Ownership]], and his [[Inner Orientation]]. It is the target his [[ROI-Driven Thinking]] aims at.
