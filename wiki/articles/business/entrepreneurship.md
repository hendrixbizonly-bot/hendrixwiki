# Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship is one of [[Hendrix]]'s core orientations. He is strongly biased toward it, not as a label, but as the default way he thinks about work. Building, owning, and scaling something are the base case. Everything else is optional.

## What it means to him

Entrepreneurship is less about the idea of starting companies and more about:

- [[Ownership]] over dependency
- [[Leverage]] over trading time for money
- Building [[Systems]] that compound
- Turning thought into action, see [[Build and Ship]]

It connects directly to his [[Business Orientation]] and his [[Identity as Builder|identity as a builder]].

## Why it fits him

He is [[ROI-Driven Thinking|ROI-driven]], [[Execution-First Mindset|execution-first]], [[Strategic Thinking|strategic]], and [[No-BS]]. That profile is not neutral. It points at [[Entrepreneurship]] more naturally than it points at most alternatives.

His exposure to [[Asian Business Culture|Asian business culture]] reinforced a practical, outcomes-focused view of work. Business was never a romantic idea. It was a serious way of operating in the world.

## How it shows up now

Currently it shows up as [[Duodode]], a [[Digital Agency]]. The agency is the vehicle. The entrepreneurial orientation is older and broader than any one venture.

## What he is careful about

- Not confusing [[Entrepreneurship]] with aesthetics or identity
- Not falling for [[Performative Business Talk]]
- Not chasing status signals over real [[Business Value]]

Entrepreneurship without substance is a hobby. Hendrix treats it as serious work.
