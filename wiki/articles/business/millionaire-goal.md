# Millionaire Goal

The millionaire goal is one of my concrete near-term ambitions. It is not a fantasy. It is a specific financial target that anchors a larger arc.

## What it means

Becoming a millionaire, for me, is a real and practical goal on the way to larger outcomes. It is a milestone that confirms the system is working: the business, the [[Systems]], the [[Leverage]], the [[Taste]], the [[Execution Over Talk|execution]].

See [[Long-term Goals]].

## Why I treat it as serious

I respect [[Money]] as a proxy for:

- [[Freedom]]
- Capability
- [[Scale]]
- Real-world proof that the thinking and building are working

A specific financial goal prevents ambition from drifting into mood.

## How it ladders

- First: build [[Duodode]] into a serious [[Digital Agency]]
- Next: reach the millionaire milestone through [[Wealth Building]], [[Productized Services]], and [[Scaling an Agency]]
- After: build toward something much larger, see [[Ambition]] and [[Agency-vs-Product]]

## What it is not

- Not a flex
- Not a destination
- Not the end of ambition

It is a checkpoint that proves the machine works, and then the machine keeps going.
