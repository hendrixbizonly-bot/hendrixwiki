# Hospitality

## What I Mean By It

Hospitality is one of those fields that reveals more than it advertises. From the outside it can look like service work. In reality, it asks for emotional control, social reading, attentiveness, and an ability to stay composed while carrying other people's needs.

## Why It Matters Here

I keep it in the archive because it connects naturally to **[[Emotional Awareness]]**. Good hospitality depends on noticing tone, tension, mood, and expectation without needing everything said out loud. It also often leans on communication skill, presentation, and sometimes **[[English]]** as well.

## The Personal Link

In this wiki, the clearest personal association is **[[Thao Phuong]]**. Her quiet nature and strong EQ made her current direction in hospitality feel believable to me rather than surprising. It fits the deeper shape I remember.

There is also a team dimension to hospitality that makes it brush against pages like **[[Team Culture]]**. The setting changes, but the human requirements overlap.
