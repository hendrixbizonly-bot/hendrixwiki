# Fashion

## Why It Belongs

Fashion belongs in this archive because visual judgment does not stop at screens, brands, or interfaces. Clothes are another place where taste becomes visible. They reveal how someone edits themselves before they say a word.

I do not treat fashion like a detached trend feed. I treat it more like an everyday expression of proportion, confidence, restraint, and self-understanding.

## What It Connects To

That is why fashion sits close to **[[Taste]]**, **[[Branding]]**, and **[[Design Philosophy]]** for me. All of them are really about decisions. What to include. What to remove. What kind of signal to send. What kind of atmosphere to create.

## The Personal Layer

This page also exists because some friendships of mine have grown through clothing conversations more than people might expect. **[[Tung]]** is one clear example. The overlap in visual instinct made the friendship feel more legible. Even people like **[[Van Anh]]**, who belong to a different part of my life, connect back to the broader question of presentation, seriousness, and social reading.
