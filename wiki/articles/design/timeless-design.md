# Timeless Design

Timeless design is the second principle in my [[Design Philosophy]]. It means the work should still hold up years after it ships. It is the antidote to trend-chasing.

## What timeless means to me

Timeless does not mean minimal, flat, or beige. It means the foundation does not rot. A timeless system can wear a trend on top without being defined by it. Strip the skin and the bones still work.

I see most trend-driven design as disposable. It wins a Dribbble like in the first month and looks dated in the second year. That is a bad return on design effort.

## The filter

Before shipping, he asks whether the decision would still feel right in three to five years. If the answer depends on a trend cycle, the decision is weak. If the answer is "yes, because the structure is sound", it stays.

## Why it matters to me

[[Duodode]] is not a trend shop. Clients pay for systems that compound, not skins that expire. Timeless design is how the agency protects its own reputation. Work that ages well keeps earning.

It also mirrors how I think about [[Money]] and [[Long-term Upside|long-term upside]]. I am not interested in quick wins that decay. I want things that last.

## How it connects

Timeless design depends on [[Intentional Design|intention]]. Trendless choices are almost always intentional ones. It also depends on [[Taste]], which is what separates timeless from merely safe.
