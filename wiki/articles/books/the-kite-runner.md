# The Kite Runner — Khaled Hosseini

A novel about friendship, guilt, betrayal, and the long reach of the past.

## Essay

The book stays alive through the way memory refuses to remain in the past when it is tied to guilt, love, and unfinished moral debt. Some actions do not end when the moment ends. They keep asking something of the self afterward.

That is why the book feels connected to [[What Lasts]] and [[Inner Life]]. I remember it as a story about the long shadow of what a person does or fails to do, and about how deeply another human being can remain inside the conscience.
