# The Book Thief — Markus Zusak

A historical novel about a young girl, books, and survival in Nazi Germany.

## Essay

This book stayed with me because it treats words as something heavier than decoration. Language can comfort, wound, survive, remember, and protect. I always respond when a story takes words seriously in that way.

It belongs close to [[Language as Thought]] and [[Mortality]] in my mind. Books can become a way of carrying humanity through conditions that try to crush it. I think that idea matters a lot, especially in a project like this one where writing itself is part of what I am using to preserve a life.
