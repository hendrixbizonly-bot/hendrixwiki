# The Tipping Point — Malcolm Gladwell

A nonfiction book about how ideas, trends, and behaviors spread through society.

## Essay

I remember this book less as a rigid theory and more as a way of seeing spread, influence, and momentum. It made me pay more attention to how small things sometimes scale through networks rather than through force.

That matters because I am already interested in [[Growth]], [[Scale]], and [[Leverage]]. The book gave that interest a more social texture. Not everything compounds because it is bigger. Sometimes it compounds because it hits the right structure at the right moment.
