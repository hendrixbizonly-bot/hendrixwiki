# The Alchemist — Paulo Coelho

A fable-like novel about a young shepherd following a journey toward his personal destiny.

## Essay

I do not keep this book for complexity. I keep it for atmosphere. It has that simple, searching quality that makes direction feel almost spiritual without becoming heavy.

The idea I keep from this book is that a life can have a signal inside it, and that paying attention to it matters. That is why the book lives next to [[Future Orientation]], [[Wonder]], and [[Becoming]] for me. Even when the language is gentle, the underlying question is serious: are you walking toward the life that is actually yours.
