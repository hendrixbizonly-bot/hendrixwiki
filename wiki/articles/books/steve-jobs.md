# Steve Jobs — Walter Isaacson

A biography of Steve Jobs covering his life, temperament, and role in shaping Apple.

## Essay

What keeps Jobs interesting to me is the fusion of intensity and taste. A lot of ambitious people know how to push. Fewer know how to push while holding a very specific standard for what something should feel like.

That is why this book matters in my world. It makes visible the connection between [[Ambition]] and [[Taste]], between product vision and the refusal to accept mediocrity as finished work. I do not need to romanticize everything about him to find that combination instructive.
