This page is the shelf for the books I would actively recommend, revisit, or point people toward when they want a cleaner picture of what shapes my thinking.

## Why this page exists

I do not want books in this archive to stay scattered across separate essays about reading. Some books matter enough to deserve a clearer shelf of their own. This category is where I can keep the strongest recommendations in one place, not just as things I have touched, but as things I would genuinely hand to someone else.

## What belongs here

The focus is not volume. It is signal. I want this page to hold books that sharpen ambition, perspective, judgment, imagination, or language. That can include fiction, philosophy, biography, strategy, or anything else that leaves a durable mark.

## How I want to use it

Over time this page should become a compact map of the books I return to, quote, or recommend most often. It is less a reading log and more a shelf of conviction.
