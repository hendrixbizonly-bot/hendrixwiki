# The Almanack of Naval Ravikant — Eric Jorgenson

A curated book of Naval Ravikant?s ideas on wealth, leverage, judgment, and living well.

## Essay

This book fits me because it sits right at the intersection of several things I care about already: leverage, wealth, freedom, judgment, and building a life that compounds rather than merely stays busy.

No single line carries the book for me. What does is the overall orientation toward smarter forms of ownership and a cleaner relationship to status games. That is why it belongs next to [[Leverage]], [[Wealth]], and [[Freedom]] in the archive. It reinforces the part of me that wants upside with real independence attached to it.
