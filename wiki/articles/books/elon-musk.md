# Elon Musk — Walter Isaacson

A biography tracing Elon Musk?s life, companies, and influence across several industries.

## Essay

What interests me in a figure like Musk is not whether I agree with everything. It is the scale of intention. I am drawn to people who make ordinary ambition look too small, especially when the work touches systems, technology, and the future at a civilizational scale.

That is why this biography connects to [[Ambition]], [[Scale]], and the [[Future of Humanity]] in my mind. It keeps the question of scope alive. What does it look like when a person aims at industries instead of projects. What does that do to the work, and what does it do to the person.
