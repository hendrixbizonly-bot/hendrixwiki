# Digital Product Work

Digital product work is one of the three pillars of [[Duodode]], alongside [[Branding]] and [[AI Automation]]. For me, it is the space where my [[Design Philosophy]] and technical range come together at full strength.

## What it means at Duodode

Digital product work covers everything involved in designing and building real products that live on the web or in applications. It includes:

- [[Web Design]] and [[Web Development]]
- [[Application Design]] and [[Application Development]]
- UI and UX systems
- Motion, interaction, and product feel
- Front-end implementation on modern stacks

The output is not a mockup. It is a working product with [[Taste]].

## Why it matters to me

I do not want to be boxed in as "just a designer" or "just a developer". Digital product work is where those labels stop mattering. The work requires [[Strategic Thinking]], [[Design]], and engineering to hold together.

It also lets me use the full stack I have built up: [[Figma]], [[VS Code]], [[GitHub]], [[Codex]], [[Claude Code]], [[Node.js]], [[Next.js]], [[Vercel]], [[Supabase]], [[Rive]], [[GSAP]], [[Framer Motion]].

## The standard

Product work at Duodode should feel intentional, consistent, and modern. Interactions should feel real. Performance should hold up. The product should do something useful, not just look good.

## How it connects

Digital product work usually starts with [[Branding]] and extends into [[AI Automation]]. The agency is designed so that one engagement can carry across all three without losing [[Clarity]] or quality. See [[Digital Agency]] and [[Design Philosophy]].
