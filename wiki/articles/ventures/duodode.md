# Duodode

Duodode is [[Hendrix]]'s current main venture. It is a [[Digital Agency]] that blends [[Branding]], [[AI Automation]], and [[Digital Product Work]] across web and applications. It is the vehicle through which his thinking about [[Design]], [[Tech]], and [[Business Strategy]] becomes real work for real clients. Everything else on this wiki eventually routes back through Duodode in some way.

## What it is

A digital agency that treats design and engineering as one system rather than two separate trades. Duodode exists because Hendrix does not see value in shallow [[Creative Services]] that stop at aesthetics, nor in engineering that ignores [[Taste]]. The venture is built around the idea that the two should compound, not coexist awkwardly.

It is a business, not a portfolio. The goal is [[Scale]], [[Leverage]], and real [[ROI]], not a showreel.

## What it does

Four service lines:

- [[Branding]]
- [[AI Automation]]
- [[Web Design]] and [[Web Development]]
- [[Application Design]] and [[Application Development]]

These are not four unrelated offerings. They are four faces of the same work: building smart, sharp digital systems that do something useful for a business.

## Positioning

Duodode is meant to feel smart, sharp, useful, and modern. It is positioned against two things Hendrix has little patience for:

- Agencies that sell aesthetics without outcomes
- Technical shops that ship features without [[Clarity]] or feel

The positioning is [[No-BS]]. Work should move the business forward. If it does not, it is decoration.

## Services in detail

- **[[Branding]]** - strategy, identity systems, and the visual language that holds a company together over time.
- **[[AI Automation]]** - using AI and tools like [[n8n]] to remove friction, compress workflows, and create [[Systems]] that give clients leverage.
- **[[Web Design]] and [[Web Development]]** - marketing sites, product sites, and web platforms built to feel intentional and perform in the real world.
- **[[Application Design]] and [[Application Development]]** - full product design and front-end build on stacks like [[Next.js]], [[Vercel]], and [[Supabase]].

The stack is deliberately modern and lean: [[Figma]], [[VS Code]], [[GitHub]], [[Codex]], [[Claude Code]], [[Node.js]], [[Next.js]], [[Vercel]], [[Supabase]], [[n8n]], [[Rive]], [[Postman]], [[Notion]], [[Ads Manager]].

## Principles

- Intentional, timeless, consistent. The [[Design Philosophy]] is not decoration, it is the filter.
- Design and tech working together, not separately.
- Not art for art's sake. Not [[Fluff|shallow agency fluff]].
- Work should create outcomes, clarity, and [[Business Value]].
- Ship real things. [[Build and Ship|Execution]] matters more than talk.

## What makes it different

Most agencies pick a lane: they are a branding shop, a dev shop, or a marketing shop. Duodode is built on the belief that the lanes are artificially separated. A brand that looks sharp but renders badly on the site it is meant to live on is a failed brand. A well-coded app with no [[Taste]] is a forgettable app.

Hendrix treats Duodode as a studio where [[Strategic Thinking]], design, and engineering happen in the same room, run by someone who cares about all three. That integration is the product.

## The vision

Duodode is the current form, not the ceiling. It is a [[Digital Agency]] now, but the long arc is larger: build real weight, scale a serious company, and eventually own something big. See [[Agency-vs-Product]], [[Scaling an Agency]], and [[Millionaire Goal]] for how this ladders up.

The underlying bet is that a sharp, focused agency with strong [[Taste]], real technical depth, and an [[AI Automation|AI-native]] way of working can compound into something much larger than traditional agency economics suggest.
