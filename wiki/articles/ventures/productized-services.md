# Productized Services

Productized services is the bridge between an [[Digital Agency|agency]] and a product. It means taking repeatable client work, packaging it into a defined offer with a fixed scope and price, and delivering it through [[Systems]] instead of custom-fitting every engagement. For me, it is one of the most obvious levers for scaling [[Duodode]] without trading sleep for revenue.

## Why it fits Duodode

- Parts of [[Branding]], [[Web Design]], and [[AI Automation]] repeat across clients. Those parts can be productized.
- Productized offers create predictable pricing, predictable delivery, and predictable [[ROI]] for both sides.
- They let the agency move from custom work as the default to custom work as the premium tier.

## What it might look like

- A branding sprint with a fixed scope and price
- A landing page or marketing site package on [[Next.js]] and [[Vercel]]
- A defined [[AI Automation]] package built on [[n8n]]
- A recurring retainer for design and automation upkeep

Nothing here is decided. These are the kinds of shapes the offer could take. See [[Scaling an Agency]].

## The ROI lens

I evaluate productization through [[ROI]] and [[Leverage]]. A productized service earns its place when it compresses delivery time, raises margin, or unlocks a new client tier, without sacrificing the quality that defines [[Duodode]].

## The risk

The risk is over-productizing. Not every client problem fits a package, and cramming it in creates weak work. Productized services should cover the repeatable layer, not replace real [[Strategic Thinking]].
