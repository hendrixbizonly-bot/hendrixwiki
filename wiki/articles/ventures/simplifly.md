# Simplifly

[[Simplifly]] belongs to the current builder chapter around [[Shaurya]]. What makes it memorable to me is not only the product itself, but the speed and seriousness with which it was being shaped.

My role around it was mainly on the design side. Helping with logo and UI work made the project one of the places where our exchange became concrete. He pushed me on technical ambition. I pushed on feel, structure, and visual direction through [[Design Philosophy]] and [[UI Design]].

That is why Simplifly matters beyond its surface. It represents a real working relationship, and another reminder that [[Build and Ship]] only becomes meaningful when two people can sharpen each other's blind spots.
