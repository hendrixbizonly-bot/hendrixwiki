# Web Development

Web development is where [[Duodode]] turns design into working product. It is the engineering side of the web work, and for me, it is where [[Taste]] meets real [[Execution Over Talk|execution]].

## Stack

- [[Next.js]] on [[Vercel]] for most modern sites and product fronts
- [[Supabase]] when a backend or database layer is needed
- [[Node.js]], [[GitHub]], [[VS Code]], [[Codex]], [[Claude Code]] for the working environment
- [[GSAP]] and [[Framer Motion]] for motion
- [[Rive]] where interactive motion benefits from it

I prefer using the right tool for the job rather than over-identifying with a framework. See [[Frontend Knowledge]].

## What it covers

- Marketing and product sites
- Content and CMS-driven sites
- Lightweight web platforms
- Integrations with analytics, [[Ads Manager]], and [[AI Automation]] pipelines

## The standard

Shipped work is the point. Clean code matters, but not as an aesthetic. Web development at Duodode is judged by whether the site ships fast, performs well, holds up under traffic, and feels right in motion.

## Why it matters at the agency

An agency that can only design pretty screens cannot finish the job. By owning development end-to-end, Duodode controls the final result. That is why web design and web development sit next to each other as paired service lines, not separate offerings.
