# AI Automation

AI automation is one of [[Duodode]]'s four service lines and one of [[Hendrix]]'s most practical bets on where leverage is moving. It is not a trend he follows. It is a layer he builds with.

## How he thinks about it

AI is leverage. Automation is leverage. AI automation is the two compounded. A client that replaces manual workflows with well-designed automated ones gets back time, cuts error rates, and opens [[Scale]] that headcount alone cannot match.

Hendrix uses [[AI Tools]] in his own work to learn faster, think more clearly, and ship more. He sees no reason businesses should not get the same advantage.

## What it covers at Duodode

- Workflow automation using [[n8n]]
- AI-powered internal tools for ops, marketing, and sales
- Integrations that connect existing stacks (CRM, data, email, docs)
- Custom AI features embedded inside client products, built on modern stacks like [[Next.js]] and [[Supabase]]

## Why it belongs inside the agency

Most branding or design shops cannot ship automation. Most automation shops have no [[Taste]]. Duodode sits in the middle: the same team that designs a clean brand and builds a sharp website also builds the AI workflow that actually moves the business. That integration is the point. See [[Digital Agency]] and [[Agency-vs-Product]].

## The ROI lens

Hendrix evaluates every automation through [[ROI]]. An automation that saves a few hours a week is a weak sell. An automation that compresses an entire function, removes a hiring need, or unlocks a new channel is real [[Leverage]]. Duodode's work is aimed at the latter.

## What he avoids

- AI work that is a demo, not a system
- "AI features" that exist for marketing, not outcomes
- Over-engineered pipelines where a simple script would do
