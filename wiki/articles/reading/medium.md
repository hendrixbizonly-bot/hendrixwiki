# Medium

Medium is part of my reading rotation alongside [[Substack]], [[The New York Times]], and standalone [[Essays]]. I use it to pull in perspectives across tech, design, business, and culture without committing to a single publication.

## What it is to me

- A broad pool of [[Essays]] across many domains
- A place to find practical writing on tech, design, and business
- A supplement to my deeper [[Books]] and [[Long-form Writing|long-form]] reading
- A quick path to voices I might otherwise miss

## How I use it

I read selectively and skim the rest. The point is not to consume Medium whole. The point is to pick up useful writing, sharper phrasing, or new angles from [[Thoughtful Creators]] in fields I care about.

## Why it fits me

I respect [[Directness]] and real ideas. Medium at its best delivers both. When it does not, I move on quickly. That filter is the same one I apply everywhere: [[Substance over Noise|substance over noise]].
