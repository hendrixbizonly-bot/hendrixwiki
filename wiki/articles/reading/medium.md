# Medium

Medium is part of [[Hendrix]]'s reading rotation alongside [[Substack]], [[The New York Times]], and standalone [[Essays]]. He uses it to pull in perspectives across tech, design, business, and culture without committing to a single publication.

## What it is to him

- A broad pool of [[Essays]] across many domains
- A place to find practical writing on tech, design, and business
- A supplement to his deeper [[Books]] and [[Long-form Writing|long-form]] reading
- A quick path to voices he might otherwise miss

## How he uses it

He reads selectively and skims the rest. The point is not to consume Medium whole. The point is to pick up useful writing, sharper phrasing, or new angles from [[Thoughtful Creators]] in fields he cares about.

## Why it fits him

Hendrix respects [[Directness]] and real ideas. Medium at its best delivers both. When it does not, he moves on quickly. That filter is the same one he applies everywhere: [[Substance over Noise|substance over noise]].
