# Jack

## Who He Is

Jack is part of the **[[Duodode]]** team and one of the people I have learned from through close collaboration. He thinks logically, learns quickly, and has a way of breaking things down that makes his mind visible very fast.

## Logic on the Team

My first impression of Jack was that he processed problems structurally. He did not just want instructions. He wanted to understand the system underneath the instruction.

That matters in team settings. It means he does not stay passive for long. Once something clicks, he moves. That is one reason I connect him to **[[Systems]]** and **[[Iteration]]** more naturally than to softer labels.

## Where We Clash and Learn

We do not think the same way, and that is part of the value. There have been moments of friction, but the friction usually produces clarity. He forces cleaner reasoning. I probably force a different kind of pressure back on him.

What makes that tension useful is that he is not only logical. He also pays attention to people. He notices emotional texture more than someone with his type of mind is often expected to. That balance is part of what makes him a meaningful person to work with.

Outside of work, he also has strong game sense. Timing, decision-making, pattern recognition. You feel it quickly.

## Shared Projects

- Worked together as part of **[[Duodode]]**.
- Talked through team decisions, product logic, and day-to-day execution.
- Played games together.
- Won a Clash League of Legends tournament with **[[Brian]]** and **[[Jake]]**.
