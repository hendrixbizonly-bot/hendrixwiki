# Viet Anh

## Who He Is

Viet Anh is one of my long-term friends and another person I lived with after university. He is one of those people whose influence comes less from dramatic moments and more from steady repetition.

## Consistency in Daily Form

If I had to place one word at the center of this page, it would be reliability. Viet Anh shows up in a way that becomes more meaningful the longer you know him.

That kind of consistency shapes how you think about a person. It also shapes how you think about yourself. Being around someone stable changes the emotional weather of a place.

## The Roommate Rhythm

Living together made that quality impossible to miss. The friendship was built into daily life: routines, conversations, small check-ins, shared space, and the ordinary pace of being around each other all the time.

One small detail says a lot about the larger pattern. We have kept a long **[[TikTok Streak]]** going, close to 600 days. It is a tiny thing, but it captures the deeper truth. He is there again tomorrow. Then again the day after that. That kind of **[[Daily Practice]]** has its own quiet weight.

## Small Proofs

- Lived together after university.
- Shared routines, conversations, and everyday life.
- Kept a long **[[TikTok Streak]]** going over hundreds of days.
- Influenced my thinking through steady presence more than dramatic advice.
