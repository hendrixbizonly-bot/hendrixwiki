# Giang

## Who He Is

Giang is one of my high school friends and is now moving through the finance world. In my memory, though, he is attached less to job titles and more to a certain kind of energy: active, funny, and easy to be around.

## Humor and Motion

We clicked early because the humor lined up. That matters more than it sounds. Some friendships become easy because the conversation never has to fight its way forward, and Giang belongs in that category for me.

He also brought a different physical energy into the group. There was always something athletic in the way he moved, a steadiness around training and activity that made him stand out next to the usual rhythm of hanging out, talking, and joking around.

## What Running Added

Running is one of the clearest things I associate with him. Seeing his consistency around physical effort made me take **[[Running]]** more seriously myself. That is a small influence on paper, but small influences often end up changing daily life more than dramatic advice does.

He belongs to the friend group that includes people like **[[Duc Anh]]** and **[[Linh Ngoc]]**, which is another way of saying he belongs to the part of my life built from repeated ordinary time. That is why he sits close to **[[What Lasts]]**.

## Shared Rhythm

- Met in high school and stayed in the same friend orbit over the years.
- Spent time together in group settings, conversations, and casual hangouts.
- Shared the kind of humor that makes friendship feel immediate.
- Ran together and added a more active layer to the friendship.
