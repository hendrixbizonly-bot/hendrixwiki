# Elvis Presley

[[Elvis Presley]] entered my life through [[John]], which is part of why the connection feels personal rather than purely musical. Some artists arrive through algorithms. Others arrive through a person, a place, and a period of life. Elvis belongs to the second kind.

What stayed with me was not only the music. It was the atmosphere around it. Bars in Hanoi, long conversations, shared drinks, and the feeling of being introduced to an older cultural world by someone who had actually lived more of it.

After that, Elvis stopped being a distant icon and became part of my own listening life. He ended up becoming my top artist on Spotify for a stretch, which says a lot about how deeply that influence landed.

I connect him naturally to [[Music]] and, more broadly, to [[American Pop Culture Influence]].
