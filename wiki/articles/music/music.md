This page is the music shelf inside Hendrixpedia: the artists, albums, and tracks that stay in rotation because they match my taste, mood, or sense of momentum.

## Why this page exists

Music shapes atmosphere faster than almost anything else. It changes how a day feels, how work lands, how ambition sounds, and what kind of emotional temperature I am carrying. That makes it worth treating as part of the archive rather than leaving it implicit.

## What belongs here

This category should collect artists and music that actually stay with me, not random playlists or passing background noise. The point is to capture taste with some seriousness.
