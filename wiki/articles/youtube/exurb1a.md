# Exurb1a

Exurb1a is a philosophical, existential video essay channel. For me, it is one of the core voices on [[YouTube]] that pushes me to think about scale, meaning, and the weirder edges of existence.

## What it is

Long-form video essays on philosophy, existence, science, and the human condition. Written with literary tone and personality. Not polished corporate content.

## Why I watch

I am drawn to [[Philosophy]], [[Long-form Thinking]], and thoughtful [[Video Essays]]. Exurb1a hits all three. It is the kind of content that expands how I frame questions, which in turn sharpens how I frame problems inside [[Duodode]] and my life.

## What it gives me

- Fresh angles on philosophy and existence
- Exposure to ideas across [[Science]], [[Space]], and being
- A voice with real personality and writing craft
- [[English]] at a literary register

## Connection to Nietzsche

My favorite philosopher is [[Nietzsche]]. Exurb1a is not a Nietzsche channel, but it shares a willingness to sit with uncomfortable questions and refuse easy answers. That matches the kind of thinking I value.

## Where it fits

Alongside [[Sisyphus 55]], [[Exurb2a]], and [[Olisunvia]], Exurb1a is part of my philosophy and essay cluster on YouTube. Light on talk, heavy on craft.

## Connection to my system

For an [[ROI-Driven Thinking|ROI-driven]] builder, philosophy is not a luxury. It is a way to stay clear on why you are doing any of this. Exurb1a feeds that clarity. See [[Philosophy]] and [[YouTube as Learning Platform]].
