# Cleo Abram

Cleo Abram's channel is optimistic tech journalism. For me, it is one of the channels that frames [[Tech]] in terms of upside and possibility rather than fear.

## What it is

Short, well-produced videos explaining current and emerging technology, with a clear frame on what they could make possible.

## Why I watch

I am a builder, not a cynic. I am using [[AI Tools]], [[AI Automation]], and other emerging tech inside [[Duodode]]. Cleo Abram's framing matches my instinct: technology is serious, and used well, it creates leverage and real change.

## What it gives me

- Current awareness of important tech moves
- Clear explanations of complex topics
- A positive, grounded frame on where things are going
- Another data point for thinking about where to place bets

## Fit in my stack

Between [[Fireship]] (developer-level), [[Joma Tech]] (career-level), [[Kurzgesagt]] (science-level), and Cleo Abram (journalism-level), I cover tech from multiple heights.

## Connection to my system
