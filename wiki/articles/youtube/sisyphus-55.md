# Sisyphus 55

Sisyphus 55 is a philosophy video essay channel. For me, whose favorite philosopher is [[Nietzsche]] and who treats philosophy as a real tool rather than decoration, it is one of the core channels in my essay cluster.

## What it is

Long-form video essays on philosophers, schools of thought, psychology, art, and how ideas connect to real life.

## Why I watch

He wants philosophy that connects to life and action, not abstract talk. Sisyphus 55 consistently works in that register: serious thinkers explained with care, tied back to how someone might actually live.

## What it gives me

- Structured entry points into philosophers and ideas
- Careful explanations without dumbing things down
- Reinforcement of [[Self-Overcoming]] and related themes
- A steady diet of [[Long-form Thinking]]

## Fit in my essay cluster

With [[Exurb1a]], [[Exurb2a]], and [[Olisunvia]], Sisyphus 55 covers my ongoing philosophy watching. Different voices, same overall purpose: keep the thinking alive.

## Connection to Nietzsche

Much of my interest in philosophy centers on [[Nietzsche]] and ideas like [[Self-Overcoming]]. Sisyphus 55 covers Nietzsche and the broader tradition in ways that sharpen how I use those ideas. See [[Philosophy]].

## Connection to my system

Philosophy for me is not entertainment. It is a tool for perspective, [[Self-Overcoming]], and stronger living. A channel that takes that seriously is high-leverage input. See [[YouTube as Learning Platform]].
