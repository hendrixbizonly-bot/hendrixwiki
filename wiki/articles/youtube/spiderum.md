# Spiderum

Spiderum is a Vietnamese intellectual content channel and platform. For me, it is one of the places my learning stays connected to my [[Vietnamese]] language and [[Vietnam|Vietnamese]] cultural context.

## What it is

Vietnamese-language content covering philosophy, psychology, culture, career, and ideas. Not clickbait. Written and produced with a serious tone.

## Why I watch

His working and thinking language has shifted heavily to [[English]]. Spiderum is a deliberate counterweight: high-quality [[Vietnamese]] thinking, still in my native language, on topics I already cares about.

## What it gives me

- Serious content in [[Vietnamese]]
- Intellectual range inside my mother tongue
- A thread back to [[Vietnam]] and local conversation
- Perspectives that do not always show up in Western content

## Why the bilingual input matters

Operating entirely in English risks losing the texture of my first language. Spiderum keeps [[Vietnamese]] alive as a medium for serious ideas, not only for family and roots. That protects [[Leverage]] I already has rather than letting it decay.

## Connection to my system

Being intellectually active in two languages is an asset. Spiderum is one of the main places I keep that asset working. See [[Vietnamese]], [[Language Learning]], and [[YouTube as Learning Platform]].
