# The Futur

The Futur is a channel about the business side of design. For me, building [[Duodode]] as a [[Digital Agency]] that blends [[Branding]], [[AI Automation]], and product work, it is one of the most directly useful channels in my rotation.

## What it is

Videos, interviews, and frameworks on how to run a creative business: pricing, positioning, selling, branding, client work, and the operational side of design careers.

## Why I watch

He does not want to be boxed in as only a designer. He sees [[Design]] as one leverage point inside a larger business system. The Futur operates at exactly that intersection. It is a direct input for how I think about:

- [[Duodode]]'s positioning
- Pricing and packaging services
- Selling without [[Fluff]]
- Turning craft into a real company

## What it gives me

- Frameworks for the business of design
- Language to use with clients
- Perspective from operators who have already built agencies
- Confidence that [[Branding]] and [[Design]] can produce real business value

## Fit with my philosophy

The channel's tone aligns with my: treat [[Design]] seriously, treat business seriously, refuse empty aesthetics. See [[Design Philosophy]] and [[Duodode]].

## Connection to my system

The Futur is basically a business school for agency builders. For someone whose current focus is [[Duodode]], that is high-leverage input. See [[YouTube as Learning Platform]].
