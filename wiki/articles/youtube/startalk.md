# StarTalk

StarTalk is Neil deGrasse Tyson's channel, a mix of space, science, and conversation. For [[Hendrix]], who is drawn to [[Space]], [[Astronomy]], and humanity's place in the universe, it is one of the natural homes for that curiosity.

## What it is

Long-form and short-form videos on space, physics, and science, often in conversation format with scientists and guests.

## Why Hendrix watches

He is interested in [[Space]], science, and ideas that combine curiosity with scale. StarTalk delivers that in a format that is conversational, accessible, and consistent.

## What it gives him

- Ongoing input on [[Astronomy]] and [[Space]]
- Contact with working scientists and their thinking
- A conversational approach to hard topics
- [[English]] at a scientific and discussion register

## Fit in his space and science cluster

- [[StarTalk]]: conversation and discussion
- [[Astrum]]: deep dives into space topics
- [[Kurzgesagt]]: beautifully animated science explainers
- [[For All Mankind]]: narrative, alternate-history space

Together they feed the same curiosity from different angles.

## Connection to his system

Hendrix likes media that produces [[Scale]], [[Wonder]], and [[Depth]]. StarTalk is a long-running source of that. It keeps the wider frame alive: a reminder that the world is bigger than any single venture, which is useful for a builder who otherwise stays tightly focused. See [[YouTube as Learning Platform]].
