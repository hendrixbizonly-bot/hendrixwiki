# Kurzgesagt

Kurzgesagt, In a Nutshell, is a science channel that pairs rigorous topics with beautiful animation. For me, it is one of the clearest examples on [[YouTube]] of what happens when [[Science]], [[Design]], and storytelling are taken seriously at once.

## What it is

Short-to-medium videos on science, space, biology, existential risk, and big ideas. Dense writing, distinctive visual design, careful sourcing.

## Why I watch

It hits several of my interests at once:

- [[Space]] and [[Astronomy]]
- Science at large
- Beautiful, [[Intentional]] [[Design]]
- Clear writing and [[Long-form Thinking]] in short format

## What it gives me

- Strong explainers on hard topics
- A design benchmark for content
- Ideas that connect [[Science]], [[Philosophy]], and scale
- A reminder that craft plus substance scales

## Design as case study

Kurzgesagt is also a lesson in what [[Intentional]], [[Consistent]] design looks like over time. The visual system is unmistakable. For someone building [[Duodode]] around [[Branding]] and [[Design Philosophy|intentional, timeless, consistent]] design, it is a live example.

## Fit in my science cluster

With [[Astrum]] (depth), [[StarTalk]] (conversation), [[Cleo Abram]] (journalism), and Kurzgesagt (explainers), my science intake covers multiple registers.

## Connection to my system

Craft plus substance, delivered consistently, at scale. That is the shape of business I respect and the shape of the work I want to produce myself. See [[Design Philosophy]] and [[YouTube as Learning Platform]].
