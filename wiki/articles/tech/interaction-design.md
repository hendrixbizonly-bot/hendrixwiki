# Interaction Design

Interaction design is how [[Hendrix]] thinks about the behavior of a product, not just its appearance. It is where design, motion, and code meet.

## His stance

- Interaction is part of the product, not a layer on top
- Feel matters as much as layout
- Every transition and state should have a reason
- Over-animated UI is as weak as under-designed UI

## How he works on it

- Defining intent in [[Figma]]
- Implementing in [[Framer Motion]], [[GSAP]], and [[Rive]]
- Working it into components inside [[Next.js]] and [[Frontend Frameworks]]
- Testing the actual feel in the browser, not in a mock

## Why it fits his design philosophy

[[Intentional Design]] requires intention at every click. [[Consistent Design]] requires interaction patterns that share a grammar. Interaction design is where those principles get proven.

## Where it shows up

- [[Duodode]] product work
- [[Branding]] systems that extend into UI
- [[Motion Design]] tied to product state
- Client deliverables that need to feel precise, not generic
