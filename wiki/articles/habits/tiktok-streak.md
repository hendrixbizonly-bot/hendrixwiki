# TikTok Streak

[[TikTok Streak]] sounds trivial on paper. That is exactly why I find it revealing. Small repetitions often say more about a relationship than big declarations do.

I connect this habit directly to [[Viet Anh]]. Keeping a streak going for hundreds of days is not heroic, but it is a form of quiet continuity. It shows up in the same emotional territory as [[Daily Practice]] and [[What Lasts]]: the small things that only become meaningful because they keep happening.

What interests me here is not the platform itself. It is the pattern. Tiny, repeated contact can carry more truth than a lot of dramatic language.
