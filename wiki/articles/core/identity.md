# Identity

Identity for Hendrix is layered, not flat. He does not reduce himself to a job title, a tool, or a single surface of work. He thinks about who he is as something with multiple coordinates: where he came from, what he is building, and what he is becoming.

## The layers

- [[Hendrix Huynh]]: the full name, the [[Origin|origin]], the [[Vietnam|Vietnamese]] background, the family line.
- [[Hendrix]]: the operator, the builder, the current working identity.
- The projection: the founder at scale he is [[Ambition|aiming toward]].

These are not separate selves. They are the same person seen from three distances.

## Identity as a builder

Hendrix resists being boxed in. He is not only a designer. He is not only a developer. He is not only a founder of a single agency. He thinks of himself as a [[Being a Builder|builder]] whose identity is defined by outputs, judgment, and range, not by a single discipline label. Design is a leverage point. Tech is a leverage point. [[Business Strategy|Strategy]] is a leverage point. The identity is the person who composes them.

## Identity as a project

He treats identity as something under active construction. [[Becoming]] is part of the picture. He reads, [[Introspection|reflects]], builds, and iterates on himself the same way he iterates on work. Who he is today is not the ceiling.

## What he rejects

- Identity built on [[Fluff|aesthetic]] without substance.
- Identity as [[Performative Business Talk|performance]].
- Identity defined narrowly by one tool, one framework, or one trend.
- Identity as a costume, rather than an ongoing practice.

## Connection to his system

Identity ties directly to [[Becoming]], [[Self-Development]], [[Being a Builder]], and [[No-BS]]. The throughline is that Hendrix wants his identity to be real, earned through output and thought, not claimed through label.
