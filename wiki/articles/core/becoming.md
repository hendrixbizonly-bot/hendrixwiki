# Becoming

Becoming is how Hendrix thinks about himself over time. He is not a fixed person he is trying to maintain. He is a person he is trying to grow into. The word is deliberately active.

## Why it matters

He cares about [[Identity|identity]], [[Competence|competence]], and [[Ambition|ambition]] as things that are being built, not things that are declared. Becoming is the inner project that runs alongside the outer project of building [[Duodode]] and whatever follows it.

## Influences on the idea

- [[Nietzsche]] and the concept of [[Self-Overcoming|self-overcoming]].
- [[Philosophy|Philosophy]] as a tool for stronger living.
- [[Introspection|Introspection]] as the ongoing audit.
- [[Long-form Thinking|Long-form content]] that expands frame.

## What it covers

- Sharpening [[English Goals|English]] toward more precise expression.
- Building [[Spanish|Spanish]] as a new capability.
- Building [[Competence|competence]] across [[Design]], [[Tech]], and [[Business Strategy|strategy]].
- Sharpening thought, taste, judgment.
- Physical base through [[Running|running]].
- Financial scale through [[Duodode]] and beyond.

## What it is not

- A motivational slogan.
- [[Fake Motivational Tone|Fake motivational]] self-talk.
- An identity label with nothing underneath.
- A hobby. It is a standing posture.

## How it meets execution

Becoming is not a passive drift into a better self. It is paired with [[Build and Ship|build and ship]] and [[Execution-First Mindset|execution]]. Growth happens through shipped reps, not just through reflection.

## Connection to his system

Becoming ties to [[Identity]], [[Self-Development]], [[Introspection]], [[Future Orientation]], [[Nietzsche]], [[Ambition]], and [[Being a Builder]]. It is the inner layer that matches the outer ambition.
