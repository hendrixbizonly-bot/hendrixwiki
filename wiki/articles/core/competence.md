# Competence

Competence is one of the values Hendrix cares about most. Not titles, not credentials, not branding. The actual ability to do the thing, produce the output, and deliver the result.

## How he thinks about it

Competence is earned, not claimed. It is built through repetition, exposure, and real output. Not through preparation alone. This is one of the reasons he leans so hard into [[Build and Ship|build and ship]] and the [[Execution-First Mindset|execution-first mindset]]: shipping is how competence actually accrues.

## Where he wants it

Across multiple surfaces, not just one:

- [[Design]], including [[Branding]] and [[UI Design|UI]]
- [[Tech]], including frontend, backend familiarity, and deployment
- [[Business Strategy]] and [[Entrepreneurship]]
- [[AI Automation]] and modern workflows
- Communication and writing ([[English Goals]])
- [[Spanish]] as a long-horizon capability

This range is why he rejects being boxed into one discipline. See [[Being a Builder]].

## What it is not

- Looking capable.
- Sounding smart in meetings.
- Collecting credentials.
- Accumulating tools without using them.
- Performance of expertise.

Competence without output is [[Fluff|fluff]].

## How he builds it

- Shipping real work.
- Learning from [[YouTube]], [[Books]], and [[AI Tools|AI]].
- Using tools in anger, not just reading about them.
- Repetition and exposure.
- [[Feedback Loops|Feedback loops]] from shipped output.

## Connection to his system

Competence ties to [[Being a Builder]], [[Becoming]], [[Self-Development]], [[Execution-First Mindset]], [[Momentum]], and [[Seriousness]]. It is the measurable by-product of the whole system working.
