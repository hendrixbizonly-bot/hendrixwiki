# Being a Builder

"Builder" is the self-concept Hendrix returns to. Not designer. Not developer. Not founder of a single thing. Builder is the wider identity that contains all of those as tools inside a larger system.

## Why this framing

A single label is too narrow. He works across [[Design]], [[Tech]], [[Business Strategy|strategy]], [[AI Automation|automation]], and [[Branding|branding]]. Calling himself only a designer flattens the picture. Calling himself only a founder ignores the hands-on output. Builder is the frame that fits.

## What the identity implies

- Output is the measure. Not role, not title.
- Surfaces compose. [[Design]] is a leverage point. [[Tech]] is a leverage point. [[Business Strategy|Strategy]] is a leverage point. The builder is the one composing them into outcomes.
- Tools are instruments, not identity. He uses [[Figma]], [[Next.js]], [[Supabase]], [[n8n]], [[Claude Code]], and the rest as leverage, not as labels.
- Range beats specialization (for him).

## What it rules out

- Being reduced to UI/UX only.
- Being framed as a content creator.
- Being framed narrowly around web only.
- Identity tied to a single framework or trend.

## Where it lives

- In [[Duodode]], which spans branding, automation, and product.
- In [[Hendrixpedia]], where he is building his own second-brain wiki.
- In [[Becoming|the longer arc]] of who he wants to be.
- In how he chooses what to learn next.

## Connection to his system

Being a builder ties to [[Identity]], [[Competence]], [[Execution-First Mindset]], [[Build and Ship]], [[Ambition]], and [[Becoming]]. It is the through-line that makes the rest of the system coherent.
