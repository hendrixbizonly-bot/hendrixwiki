# No-BS

No-BS is a core part of how Hendrix operates. It describes his intolerance for [[Fluff|fluff]], empty talk, and motion that does not translate into real work. It is not performed roughness. It is a filter.

## What it means

Every claim has to be backed by something real. Every plan has to connect to [[Execution-First Mindset|execution]]. Every piece of communication has to say something useful. The moment language starts dressing up nothing, his attention pulls away.

## Where he applies it

- In communication: prefers [[Directness|direct]], [[Concise Writing|concise]], [[Natural Language|natural]] phrasing. No corporate bloat.
- In work: real outputs over polished decks that never ship.
- In branding: [[Duodode]] is intentionally no-BS in its positioning: smart, sharp, useful, modern.
- In thinking: values grounded ideas over [[Try-hard Language|try-hard]] or [[Fake Motivational Tone|fake motivational]] framing.
- In self-talk: [[Introspection]] is honest, not flattering.

## What he rejects

- [[Performative Business Talk]]
- [[Fake Work]]
- [[Fluff]]
- Hype language with no substance
- Aesthetics that do not serve outcomes
- Em-dashes in writing help
- Endless planning disguised as progress

## No-BS is not harsh

It is not about being blunt for the sake of it. It is about respecting the reader, the client, the collaborator, and himself enough to skip the costume. Clear, grounded, useful. That is the tone.

## Connection to his system

No-BS ties to [[Clarity]], [[Directness]], [[Seriousness]], [[Execution-First Mindset]], and [[ROI-Driven Thinking]]. It is the temperament that keeps the rest of the system from drifting into show.
