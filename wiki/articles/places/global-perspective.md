# Global Perspective

Global perspective is a natural by-product of how Hendrix came up. [[Vietnam|Vietnamese]] origin, [[Dubai|UAE]] base, [[English|English]] fluency, heavy exposure to [[Series Influences|American and English-language series]], and regular contact with [[San Francisco|tech culture]] through media. The result is someone whose default frame is not one region.

## What it means in practice

- Thinking in multiple markets and audiences, not only one.
- Moving easily across cultural and linguistic surfaces.
- Noticing context-specific assumptions and separating them from universal ones.
- Positioning [[Duodode]] for clients across multiple regions.
- Absorbing ideas from wherever they come: American tech, European design, Asian business culture, global philosophy.

## How it shows up in Duodode

[[Duodode]] is built to feel [[Design Philosophy|intentional, timeless, and consistent]] across markets. It is not positioned narrowly for one city. That is a direct reflection of the builder's frame.

## Inputs that built it

- [[Vietnam]] as origin.
- [[Dubai]] as operating base.
- [[English]] as a working language across cultures.
- [[Spanish]] being added as a third axis.
- [[Series Influences|Series]] as cultural input.
- [[YouTube]] and [[Books]] as continuous global input.

## What it is not

- Rootlessness.
- A lack of identity.
- A brand claim.

It is the working frame of someone who has lived across contexts and paid attention.

## Connection to his system

Global perspective ties to [[Dubai]], [[Vietnam]], [[San Francisco]], [[English]], [[Series Influences]], and [[Duodode]]. It is the wide-angle lens under the operating stack.
