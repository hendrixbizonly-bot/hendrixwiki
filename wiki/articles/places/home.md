# Home

Home for Hendrix is not a single address. It is layered between [[Vietnam]], where he grew up, and [[Dubai]], where he currently operates from. The word holds both, not one at the expense of the other.

## Two layers

- [[Vietnam]]: the [[Origin|origin]]. The language, family, early context, and cultural baseline.
- [[Dubai]]: the current [[Base Location|base]]. Where the work, the life, and [[Duodode|the building]] happen now.

He does not treat one as real home and the other as temporary. They hold different roles. Origin-home and operating-home.

## Home as something practical

For a builder, home is also the physical base that supports the work. Quiet enough to think. Connected enough to operate. Positioned well enough to reach clients and markets. That is the practical side of [[Base Location|base location]].

## Home as something identity-level

Home is also part of [[Identity]]. It is tied to who he is, where he came from, and how he carries himself. The [[Asian Business Culture|Asian business culture]] baseline and the Vietnamese thread are not left behind because he moved. They travel with him.

## What he avoids

- Treating home as only sentimental.
- Treating home as only logistical.
- Pretending the move erased the origin.
- Pretending the origin dictates the future.

## Connection to his system

Home ties to [[Vietnam]], [[Dubai]], [[Origin]], [[Base Location]], [[Identity]], and [[Becoming]]. It is the personal layer of place, underneath the operational layer.
