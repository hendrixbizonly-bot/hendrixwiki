# UAE

The UAE is the country that contains my base. [[Dubai]] is the specific city, but the UAE as a whole is the operating environment: its infrastructure, policy, and positioning in the global map are part of why being based there works for me.

## What the UAE offers a builder

- A government stance that is generally friendly to business, [[Entrepreneurship|entrepreneurship]], and foreign operators.
- Strong digital and physical infrastructure.
- Proximity to multiple continents, which fits my [[Global Perspective|global perspective]].
- A business culture that rewards fast movement and [[Execution-First Mindset|execution]].

## UAE versus Dubai in the wiki

[[Dubai]] is the city article: what this specific place is to me day to day. UAE is the country frame: the larger operating context. They overlap, but it is useful to keep them separate because Dubai is the tactical location, and UAE is the strategic container.

## How I use the frame

- Thinking about the UAE when evaluating long-term positioning.
- Thinking about Dubai when evaluating day-to-day operation.
- Using both as part of how I positions [[Duodode]] to clients who care about location and regulatory setting.

## What it is not

Not an identity marker. Not something I am leaning on for aesthetic. It is a base country that fits the work I am doing.
