# Quranic Park

[[Quranic Park]] is one of those Dubai places that matters because of the people attached to it. I do not think of it as a landmark first. I think of it as a setting for long walks, builder conversations, and the quieter parts of friendship.

It sits inside the same social orbit as [[Shaurya]], [[Roshan]], and [[Shifa]]. That mix says a lot already: ambition, humor, reflection, movement, and the kind of evenings where a place becomes memorable because the conversation keeps unfolding.

Some places become important because something official happened there. Quranic Park matters to me because ordinary time there kept turning into something more memorable than expected.
