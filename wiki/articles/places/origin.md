# Origin

Origin, for Hendrix, is the set of coordinates he came from before he became the builder based in [[Dubai]]. It is [[Vietnam]], [[Asian Business Culture|Asian business culture]], [[Hendrix Huynh|the family name]], and the language and instincts that came with them.

## Why origin is its own article

He does not treat origin as background noise or as a fixed past. It is one of the layers that keeps showing up in how he thinks and works. Calling it out as its own concept keeps it visible instead of folding it invisibly into "bio."

## What origin contains for him

- [[Vietnam]] as the country of upbringing.
- [[Vietnamese]] as first language.
- [[Asian Business Culture]] as a shaping context.
- Family and [[Hendrix Huynh|surname]] as a thread back.
- A practical, effort-heavy view of work as the default.

## Origin and becoming

Origin is fixed. [[Becoming]] is not. Origin is the starting point. [[Dubai]], [[Duodode]], and what comes after are the current and future points on the same line. He holds both without conflict.

## What origin is not for him

- A costume to perform.
- A limitation.
- Something to distance from.
- The whole story.

## Connection to his system

Origin ties to [[Vietnam]], [[Hendrix Huynh]], [[Asian Business Culture]], [[Home]], [[Identity]], and [[Becoming]]. It is the root under the rest of the tree.
