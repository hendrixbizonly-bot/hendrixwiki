# Origin

Origin, for me, is the set of coordinates I came from before I became the builder based in [[Dubai]]. It is [[Vietnam]], [[Asian Business Culture|Asian business culture]], [[Hendrix Huynh|the family name]], and the language and instincts that came with them.

## Why origin is its own article

I do not treat origin as background noise or as a fixed past. It is one of the layers that keeps showing up in how I think and work. Calling it out as its own concept keeps it visible instead of folding it invisibly into "bio."

## What origin contains for me

- [[Vietnam]] as the country of upbringing.
- [[Vietnamese]] as first language.
- [[Asian Business Culture]] as a shaping context.
- Family and [[Hendrix Huynh|surname]] as a thread back.
- A practical, effort-heavy view of work as the default.

## Origin and becoming

Origin is fixed. [[Becoming]] is not. Origin is the starting point. [[Dubai]], [[Duodode]], and what comes after are the current and future points on the same line. I hold both without conflict.

## What origin is not for me

- A costume to perform.
- A limitation.
- Something to distance from.
- The whole story.

## Connection to my system

Origin ties to [[Vietnam]], Hendrix Huynh, [[Asian Business Culture]], [[Home]], [[Identity]], and [[Becoming]]. It is the root under the rest of the tree.
