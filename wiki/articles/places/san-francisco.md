# San Francisco

San Francisco is not a place Hendrix lives. It is a cultural and mental reference point. Through series like [[Silicon Valley]], through [[YouTube]] creators, through tech news, through company stories, San Francisco is where a large chunk of his imagined tech canon lives.

## What it represents to him

- The symbolic capital of modern tech and startup culture.
- The home of the archetype of the builder-founder he half-relates to.
- A source of [[Global Perspective|global perspective]] on what building at scale can look like.
- A lens through which American tech culture became familiar to him.

## Absorbed, not lived

He absorbed San Francisco mostly through:

- [[Silicon Valley]] the series.
- Tech [[YouTube]] channels and essays.
- Company histories and founder stories.
- General [[Series Influences|series influences]] and pop culture.

This is a common pattern for him: a place becomes part of his mental furniture through long exposure to its stories before any physical contact.

## What he takes from it, and does not

- Takes: the ambition of building at real scale, the belief that tech can compound massively, the familiarity with startup language.
- Does not take: the performative side, the pitch-deck theater, the hype without [[Execution-First Mindset|execution]].

His actual [[Base Location|base]] is [[Dubai]], and that is deliberate.

## Connection to his system

San Francisco ties to [[Silicon Valley]], [[Series Influences]], [[Global Perspective]], [[Dubai]], and [[Ambition]]. It is a cultural input, not a location he operates from.
