# Notion

Notion is where I keep docs, notes, and operational structure for [[Duodode]] and my broader work. It is my default surface for organization.

## What it is

A flexible document and database tool. Pages, databases, and lightweight workflow inside one workspace.

## How I use it

- Internal docs for [[Duodode]]
- Client briefs, project structure, and deliverables
- Personal notes and reference material
- Tracking ongoing work without drowning in project management overhead
- Serving as a single source of truth for how the agency runs

## Why it fits my workflow

I want organization that does not become its own job. Notion is flexible enough to match my thinking and minimal enough to stay out of the way. That matches my preference for tools that serve the work, not the other way around.

## What it unlocks for me

- A stable home for how [[Duodode]] operates
- Fast search across everything I have written down
- A clean place to turn [[Introspection|reflection]] into structure
- Less mental load spent remembering where things live
