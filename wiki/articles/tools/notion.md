# Notion

Notion is where [[Hendrix]] keeps docs, notes, and operational structure for [[Duodode]] and his broader work. It is his default surface for organization.

## What it is

A flexible document and database tool. Pages, databases, and lightweight workflow inside one workspace.

## How Hendrix uses it

- Internal docs for [[Duodode]]
- Client briefs, project structure, and deliverables
- Personal notes and reference material
- Tracking ongoing work without drowning in project management overhead
- Serving as a single source of truth for how the agency runs

## Why it fits his workflow

Hendrix wants organization that does not become its own job. Notion is flexible enough to match his thinking and minimal enough to stay out of the way. That matches his preference for tools that serve the work, not the other way around.

## What it unlocks for him

- A stable home for how [[Duodode]] operates
- Fast search across everything he has written down
- A clean place to turn [[Introspection|reflection]] into structure
- Less mental load spent remembering where things live
