# DaVinci Resolve

DaVinci Resolve is [[Hendrix]]'s video editing tool. He uses it for the video side of [[Duodode]] and any personal video work. It is a full editing, color, and post suite, and it is powerful enough to grow into as his output scales.

## What it is

A professional video editing and color grading application. One of the few tools that can handle everything from simple cuts to high-end color and finishing in a single app.

## How Hendrix uses it

- Editing marketing and brand videos for [[Duodode]] clients
- Color grading to hit a consistent visual standard
- Producing content that plugs into [[Ads Manager]] campaigns
- Cutting personal or experimental video work

## Why it fits his workflow

He wants tools with real depth. Resolve has it. Starting on a serious editor means he does not have to switch later as his needs grow. That matches his preference for [[Leverage|leverage]] and [[Long-term Upside|long-term]] choices.

## What it unlocks for him

- High-quality video output without paying for multiple tools
- A real path from edit to final color without exporting between apps
- Capability for client work that needs more than a quick cut
- Another surface where his [[Design Philosophy|intentional, timeless, consistent]] standard applies
