# Crypto

## What It Means In My Archive

Crypto sits in my archive more as a living market than as an ideology. I do not keep it here because of internet mythology around it. I keep it here because it became one of the clearest doorways into market thinking through real conversations with people around me.

## Why It Matters

What makes crypto interesting is not only the asset class itself. It is the intensity around uncertainty. Volatility forces people to reveal what kind of thinkers they are. Some become reckless. Some become patient. Some discover that they never had a risk framework at all.

That is why I connect crypto to **[[Trading]]**, **[[Ambition]]**, and **[[Seriousness]]** more naturally than to hype.

## The Human Link

For me, the strongest human association is **[[Hoang Duc]]**. His long-standing interest in crypto made the subject feel less distant and more structurally understandable. It also linked crypto to the more serious, real-world side of **[[Entrepreneurship]]** rather than online noise.
