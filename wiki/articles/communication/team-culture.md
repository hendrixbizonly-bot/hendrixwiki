# Team Culture

## What It Actually Is

Team culture is the atmosphere that forms when people keep working together long enough for habits to become a social climate. It is not the values slide. It is not the slogan. It is the felt experience of being inside the team.

That experience gets shaped by tone, responsiveness, humor, standards, conflict handling, and whether people make each other sharper or more tired.

## Why I Care About It

I care about team culture because output is never only output. The environment underneath it changes how good people stay good. A strong culture helps standards survive pressure. A bad one makes even simple work heavier than it should be.

It also depends deeply on **[[Internal Communication]]**. If people cannot read what is happening around them, culture starts turning into guesswork and tension.
