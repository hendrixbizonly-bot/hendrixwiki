# Scale and Depth

Scale and depth is the register [[Hendrix]] is naturally drawn to. Big ideas, long timescales, serious thinkers, and work that actually holds up under scrutiny. He prefers media, people, and projects that operate in this register over anything shallow or trendy.

## What he means by it

- Scale: size of idea, size of ambition, size of timeline
- Depth: real substance, real thinking, real craft

Both are required. Scale without depth is marketing. Depth without scale is hobby work. He wants both.

## Where he finds it

- [[Space]] and [[Astronomy]]
- [[Science Fiction]] books
- Serious [[Philosophy]], especially [[Nietzsche]]
- [[Video Essays]] from [[Thoughtful Creators]]
- [[Long-form Writing]] in [[Essays]], [[Substack]], and [[The New York Times]]

## How it shapes his taste

Hendrix filters creators, books, and work through this lens. If something is only surface aesthetic, he loses interest fast. If something carries real weight, he stays with it. It is the same reason he avoids [[Fluff]] and [[Performative Business Talk]].

## How it shapes his building

[[Duodode]] is built to have both. Enough scale to matter as a business. Enough depth to feel [[Intentional Design|intentional]] and hold up over time. His [[Design Philosophy|design philosophy]] of intentional, timeless, consistent is a direct expression of this value.
