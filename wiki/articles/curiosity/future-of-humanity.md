# Future of Humanity

The future of humanity is one of the questions [[Hendrix]] keeps an open tab on. It sits at the intersection of technology, [[Space]], [[Philosophy]], and [[Ambition]]. For a builder focused on the [[Long-term Upside|long view]], the question is not abstract. It is part of how he thinks about direction.

## Why it interests him

- It pulls together everything he cares about: science, philosophy, technology, meaning
- It forces long-term thinking instead of quarterly thinking
- It is the natural home for [[Existential Reflection]]
- It sharpens the case for [[Ambition]] that is not small

## Where he encounters it

- [[Science Fiction]] books and series like [[For All Mankind]]
- [[Video Essays]] from creators like [[Kurzgesagt]], [[Cleo Abram]], [[Olisunvia]], [[Sisyphus 55]]
- [[Substack]] and [[The New York Times]] coverage of AI, climate, space, and tech
- [[AI Tools]] as a real, daily data point about where things are going

## How it shapes his building

Watching the future unfold in real time reinforces Hendrix's bias toward tools, systems, and companies that compound. [[Duodode]] is being built inside an economy where [[AI Automation]], [[Automation Workflows]], and [[AI-Assisted Development]] are already changing what a small team can do. His job is to stay sharp enough to keep using that leverage.

## The personal angle

He does not want to be a spectator to the next decade. The question of where humanity is going is, for him, partly a question about what he personally intends to build inside it.
