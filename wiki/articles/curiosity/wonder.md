# Wonder

Wonder is a value [[Hendrix]] keeps alive on purpose. It is one of the inputs that keeps his ambition honest and his thinking sharp. Without it, building becomes mechanical. With it, the same work carries weight.

## Why he protects it

Hendrix is [[ROI-Driven Thinking|ROI-driven]] and serious about outcomes, but he does not want that to collapse into a narrow, joyless operator mode. Wonder is how he stays curious enough to keep learning, stretching, and noticing things other people miss.

## Where it comes from

- [[Space]] and [[Astronomy]]
- [[Science Fiction]] books
- [[Video Essays]] from [[Thoughtful Creators]]
- [[Philosophy]] and existential questions
- [[Running]] and the space it creates for thinking

## What it is not

It is not cheap awe. It is not a moodboard. It is not the fake-deep tone he dislikes across [[Communication Preferences|communication]] and [[Writing Preferences|writing]]. Real wonder is quiet, grounded, and useful.

## How it shows up in his work

Wonder is one reason he keeps his creative taste sharp. It is why [[Duodode]] is meant to feel [[Intentional Design|intentional]] rather than generic. Work built without any sense of wonder tends to be forgettable, and forgettable work fails his standard of [[Substance over Noise|substance over noise]].
