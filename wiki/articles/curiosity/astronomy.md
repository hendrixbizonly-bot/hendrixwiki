# Astronomy

Astronomy is one of the lenses [[Hendrix]] uses to think beyond his immediate life. It is less about memorizing star systems and more about keeping a working sense of where everything actually sits. For him, astronomy is the most literal form of [[Scale and Depth|scale and depth]].

## Why it pulls him in

Hendrix is drawn to things that stretch perspective without cheap drama. Astronomy delivers that by default. Every real fact about it is bigger than the last. For someone who values [[Clarity]] and [[Seriousness]], astronomy is a discipline that rewards both.

## What he gets from it

- A true sense of [[Scale and Depth|scale]]
- A grounded source of [[Wonder]]
- A natural bridge into [[Science and Philosophy]]
- Raw material for [[Existential Reflection]]
- Context for how he thinks about [[Humanity's Place in the Universe|humanity's place]]

## How he engages with it

Through [[YouTube]] channels, [[Video Essays]], and long-form content. [[Astrum]], [[Kurzgesagt]], [[StarTalk]], and [[Cleo Abram]] sit inside this orbit. [[Science Fiction]] books extend the same instinct in a different register.

## Astronomy as a builder's tool

Astronomy quietly sharpens his [[Long-term Upside|long-term]] thinking. When the baseline timescale of a subject is millions of years, shallow targets start to feel uninteresting. It reinforces his bias toward [[Ambition]] with real weight and [[Execution Over Talk|real output]].
