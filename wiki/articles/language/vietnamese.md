# Vietnamese

Vietnamese is [[Hendrix]]'s native language and the language of his origin. It is the first layer of how he thinks, feels, and connects to [[Vietnam]]. While [[English]] has become his working language for [[Entrepreneurship]], [[Tech]], and global output, Vietnamese remains the private substrate underneath.

## Native ground

He grew up in Vietnam, which means Vietnamese is not a learned language for him. It is the language he absorbed before he could choose. It shapes instinct, humor, family, and the cultural texture he carries even while operating abroad from [[Dubai]].

## What it carries

Vietnamese carries things English cannot easily reach for him:

- Family and personal relationships
- [[Asian Business Culture]] nuance
- Native humor and timing
- A connection to how people actually live and speak back home

## Relationship to English

He does not treat Vietnamese and [[English]] as competitors. They cover different parts of his life. Vietnamese is origin, roots, and internal texture. English is ambition, output, and reach. Together they give him range.

## Intellectual Vietnamese content

He reads and watches Vietnamese thinking too. [[Spiderum]] is one of the channels that matters here, offering Vietnamese intellectual content on philosophy, psychology, culture, and ideas. It keeps his intellectual life bilingual rather than outsourced entirely to English.

## Connection to Dubai

Living in [[Dubai]] and building in English does not mean erasing Vietnamese. If anything, distance sharpens his awareness of it. The language is part of the identity he carries into every room.

## Connection to his system

Being bilingual is [[Leverage]]. It means two channels of input, two cultural lenses, and access to both Vietnamese and global conversations. In a world where most founders operate in one language, two is an asset.
