# Spanish

Spanish is the language [[Hendrix]] is currently learning. It is his active language project, studied daily through [[Duolingo]], and anchored by a 350 plus day [[Duolingo Streak]]. For him, Spanish is less about any one end state and more about keeping a long compounding habit alive.

## Why Spanish

Spanish is one of the largest languages in the world and sits next to [[English]] as a second global axis. Adding it expands his reach, his cultural range, and his [[Leverage]]. He already operates fluently in [[English]] and natively in [[Vietnamese]]; Spanish is a deliberate third layer.

## How he learns it

His primary tool is [[Duolingo]]. It is daily, small, and consistent, which matches how he prefers to build skills: [[Repetition]], [[Consistency]], and real [[Execution]] over heavy theory. See [[Language Learning]].

## The streak

The 350 plus day [[Duolingo Streak]] is not about gamification. It is evidence of how he treats self-development: as a long, quiet compounding project rather than a sprint. Missing a day is not just missing Spanish practice. It is breaking a promise to himself. See [[Duolingo Streak]].

## Philosophy of the project

Spanish fits his broader pattern:

- Start before feeling ready
- Show up daily
- Let competence accumulate through exposure
- Trust the compounding

It mirrors [[Build and Ship]] applied to language instead of code. He is not waiting to feel fluent before practicing; he is practicing so fluency can eventually happen.

## Connection to his system

Spanish is a long bet on his own range. It is also proof that he applies his own principles to himself, not only to [[Duodode]] or work. A third language is optionality: markets, people, culture, and media he could not otherwise access.
