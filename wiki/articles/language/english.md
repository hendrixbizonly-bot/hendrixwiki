# English

English is the language I think, writes, and builds in. I am fluent across reading, writing, listening, and speaking, but I treat it less as a finished skill and more as a surface I keep sharpening. For me, English is the working language of [[Entrepreneurship]], [[Tech]], [[Design]], and serious [[Long-form Thinking]], and how well he handles it directly affects how precisely I can think, sell, write, and lead.

## Where it came from

English was not handed to me in a Vietnamese classroom. A large part of it was absorbed through [[American Pop Culture Influence|American and English-language TV]], books, and the internet. Shows like [[How I Met Your Mother]], [[The Big Bang Theory]], [[Silicon Valley]], and later [[Mad Men]] and [[Succession]] trained my ear for rhythm, slang, timing, and how people actually talk. During high school he deliberately used series to push my English further. See [[American Pop Culture Influence]].

## Current state

I read, writes, listens, and speaks fluently. He operates in English across business, technical documentation, writing, thinking, and conversation. The foundation is solid. The work now is not basic fluency. It is refinement.

## What I want next

I want to become more [[Eloquence|eloquent]] and more natural. Specifically he wants:

- Stronger [[Articulation]]
- Sharper phrasing
- More [[Precision in Speech|precise self-expression]]
- Better command when expressing complex thoughts aloud

See [[English Goals]].

## Relationship to thinking

English is not just communication. It is the medium in which he structures thought. A sharper English means sharper writing, clearer pitches, better decisions on brand copy, and cleaner arguments in my own head. The same reason he cares about [[Clarity]] and [[Directness]] in [[Communication Preferences|communication]] is the reason he cares about my English getting better.

## Connection to building

[[Duodode]] operates in English. Client conversations, [[Branding]] work, copy, proposals, and positioning all run on it. Better English directly increases my [[Leverage]]. My view is practical: language quality is business quality when the business sells ideas.

## How I use media to keep sharpening

He still treats [[TV Series]], [[YouTube]], [[Books]], [[Essays]], [[Substack]], [[Medium]], and [[The New York Times]] as ongoing English training as much as content. Good writing raises my floor. Sharp dialogue raises my ceiling.

## Contrast with Vietnamese

My native [[Vietnamese]] is the language of origin and family. English is the language of ambition and output. They are not in competition. They live in different slots in my life. See [[Vietnamese]].
