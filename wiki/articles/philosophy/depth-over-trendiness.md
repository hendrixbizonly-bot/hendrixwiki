# Depth Over Trendiness

Depth over trendiness is one of my default filters. When I choose what to read, what to watch, who to learn from, and what to take seriously, I pick depth. Trend value decays fast. Depth compounds.

## What this means in practice

- [[Books]] over hot takes
- [[Serious Thinkers]] over popular ones
- [[Long-form Thinking]] over short clips
- Timeless ideas over current noise
- Real craft over aesthetic trend

It does not mean ignoring the present. It means weighting the deep stuff more heavily than the fashionable stuff.

## Why this filter exists

I do not want my thinking shaped by whatever is loudest this week. I want a [[Worldview]] that holds up. Trendiness pushes attention toward whatever is momentarily high-engagement, which is not the same as whatever is actually true or useful.

## The trade-off he accepts

Deep material is slower. It is less socially rewarding. He accepts both. The payoff is a mind that does not have to be rewired every six months because the last trend hollowed out.

## How it shows up across my life

- In [[Philosophy]] he reaches for [[Nietzsche]], not viral quotes
- In [[Building]] he cares about real output, not what is trending on Twitter
- In [[Design]] I prefer [[Timeless Design|timeless]] work over surface trends
- In [[Money]] I think in [[Long-term Upside]], not get-rich fast patterns
- In [[Intellectual Life]] I build slowly, through depth

## The connection to my building system

Depth over trendiness is the same pattern as [[Substance Over Noise]] and [[Real Output|real output]] over vanity. It is how I protects attention and keeps [[Duodode]] anchored in work that creates actual [[Business Value]], not aesthetic churn.
