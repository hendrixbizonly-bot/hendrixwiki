# Substance Over Noise

Substance over noise is a baseline filter in how I operate. Across ideas, work, people, and content, I pick the thing with real weight behind it and rejects the thing that is dressed-up empty.

## What it means

Substance is the underlying truth or value of a thing. Noise is decoration, performance, or volume. Most of the content in the world is noise. Most of the business talk is noise. A smaller set of things is substance. I want my attention and my output on the substance side.

## Where I apply it

- In [[Reading]]: [[Serious Thinkers]] over trending takes
- In [[Building]]: [[Real Output]] over discussion
- In [[Design]]: [[Intentional Design|intentional]] and [[Timeless Design|timeless]] over trendy
- In [[Money]]: real outcomes over appearances
- In [[Communication Preferences|communication]]: [[Directness|direct]] over performative
- In business: [[Duodode]] creating actual [[Business Value]] over shallow agency fluff

## Why it is a filter and not a preference

A preference is aesthetic. A filter is a default rule. Substance over noise is a filter. When something is loud and impressive but hollow, I do not just notice. He drops it. That is how I protects my time and my [[Mental Edge]].

## Related frictions

This is the mirror image of my frictions with [[Fluff]], [[Fake Work]], [[Performative Business Talk]], and [[Low-Value Activity]]. All of those are noise wearing the clothes of substance.

## The relationship to [[No-BS]]

Substance over noise is the content version of [[No-BS]]. No-BS is about tone and honesty. Substance over noise is about what you give your attention to. They are the same instinct expressed in different layers.

## How it shows up in my building

[[Duodode]] is positioned around this. I do not want to build an agency that produces beautiful-but-useless work. I want work that is both sharp and real. That is the filter applied at the business level.
