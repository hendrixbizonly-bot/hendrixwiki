# Friendship

## What It Means Here

Friendship in this archive is rarely about one grand declaration. It is usually about repetition. Shared routines, recurring jokes, being around in different seasons of life, and the quieter proof that someone still belongs when there is nothing dramatic forcing the connection.

## Why I Keep Returning To It

I keep returning to friendship because so much of a life gets shaped by people who were simply there often enough to matter deeply. That is part of what ties it to **[[What Lasts]]**. The weight comes from accumulation.

## The Environments That Built It

Pages like **[[High School]]** matter because they created the conditions for friendship to become visible over time. People like **[[Giang]]**, **[[Quang Tran]]**, **[[Huong Lan]]**, and **[[Viet Anh]]** each represent different forms of that repeated presence.
